About Nature

Nature is the most beautiful gift of God. Trees, rivers, mountains, and animals make our earth green and peaceful.Nature is the physical world and all living and non-living things in it, existing independently of human creation. It encompasses the entirety of the universe's phenomena, including mountains, rivers, oceans, plants, and animals, as well as the natural forces like gravity, weather, and climate that govern it. Additionally, "nature" can refer to a person's inherent character, disposition, or qualities, as in "human nature" or "good-natured".
